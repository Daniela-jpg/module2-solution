Beef

Carne asada is a dish of grilled and sliced beef, usually skirt steak, sirloin steak, tenderloin steak, or rib steak. It is usually cooked with a marinade and some searing to impart a charred flavor. Carne asada can be served as a main dish or as an ingredient in other dishes,enjoy very tasty.

Sushi

Sushi is a dish of Japanese cuisine that originates from an ancient technique of preserving fish meat in poached rice. Sushi, as it is known today, is about 200 years old; initially, it was sold in barado with vinegar sauce, sugar and salt, combined with some type of fish or seafood, vegetables or egg.
